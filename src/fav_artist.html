Kenshi Yonezu

Kenshi Yonezu

Kenshi Yonezu is a Japanese singer-songwriter, musician, and producer. He began his career as a Vocaloid music producer, creating music using the voice synthesizer software. In 2012, he made his debut as a solo artist with the single "Santa Maria". Since then, he has released several albums and singles, including "Lemon", which became the best-selling single in Japan in 2018. Yonezu's music often incorporates elements of rock, pop, and electronic music.
